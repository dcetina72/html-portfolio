The Best Movies According to Danny

My top 5 movies of all-time, bitch.

Kill Bill vol. 1 and 2

Badass lady assassins, swords, kung fu, and vengeance. Yes, both films count as one.

Hard Candy

Elliot Page is astonishing in this provocative game of cat and mouse with a surprising twist.

Kangaroo Jack

It's probably the worst film on this list, but Jerry O'Connel was my sexual awakening here, so there you go.

Turning Red

Probably one of the funniest animated films I've seen in recent years. It hits my millennial heart so hard like a runaway dump truck.

God's Own Country

A haiku on why I love this film so much:

This film is so gay
Sad farmer meets hot new guy
And no one dies. Yay!
